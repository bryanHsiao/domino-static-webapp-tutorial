建議維護方式Suggested Maintenance Pattern

後續如果還要擴充，建議維持雙軌結構： 展示頁放在 /stepX/，說明頁放在 /docs/。 這樣教學內容會清楚很多，也比較不容易在改版時失去脈絡。 For future expansion, keep two parallel tracks: showcase pages under /stepX/ and explanation pages under /docs/. This keeps the tutorial clearer and preserves the reasoning behind each stage.
